Arpanet

  • ARPAnet opened to universities in1970s, and early takers were the big universities
  • PTO hired Vinton Cerf of Stanford. Cerf and his students made the first protocol used on ARPANET, NCP.
  • Ultimately he led the INTERNET project, which included packet radio, packet satellite, and network security.
  • 1973- Cerf and his group made TCP/IP, Transmission Control Protocol/ Internet Protocol.
  • In 1976 the DoD experimented with this new protocol and eventually mandated it for ARPANET
  • January 1983- All devices connected to ARPANET utilized TCP/IP.
  • 1981- IBM created the BITNET protocol to allow E- mail and mailing lists.
  • CSNET was created by NSF as a way to allow CS departments to work together.
